Keyboard and Mouse control for Two player

Mouse controls your Remote Controlled Friend (RCF).

Cursor keys control your jetpack.
Up is thrust "forward" which is up.
Left/Right rotate.
Down slows you down, slowly.

Switch Levels:
Number keys

CHEAT KEYS:
r - Stops rotation if you are getting sick.
h - Adds insane health to your character if you want to pretend you are Batman.
